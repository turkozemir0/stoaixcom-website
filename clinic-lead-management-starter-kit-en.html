Problem

If no one knows who is following up, when they are following up, or what status a lead is in, the issue is not lead volume. It is system design.

Many clinics run lead management through memory, WhatsApp, scattered notes, and spreadsheets. That works briefly. Then warm opportunities start getting forgotten.

Scattered

Lead information lives in too many places

Calls, chats, notes, and follow-up tasks get separated, so nobody sees the full picture clearly.

Unclear

Status logic is missing

Without a clear definition of new, warm, waiting, or lost, prioritisation breaks down.

Forgotten

Follow-up is not systematic

If nobody owns the next step, warm leads do not stay warm for long.
