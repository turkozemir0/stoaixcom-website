Frequently asked questions

How does an AI receptionist work for a dermatology clinic?

STOAIX connects to your existing phone number. When a patient calls, the AI answers within 2 seconds in natural British English, handles the enquiry, and books directly into your calendar. You receive a full transcript and recording of every call. No hardware or technical setup required.

Can the AI handle different types of dermatology enquiries?

Yes. STOAIX is configured for your clinic's specific services — mole checks, acne treatment, eczema consultations, skin cancer screenings, cosmetic dermatology and more. It understands your treatments, pricing, and availability, and books the appropriate appointment type.

What happens if a patient mentions an urgent skin concern?

STOAIX can be configured to handle urgency appropriately. For potentially urgent concerns such as rapidly changing moles or suspicious lesions, the AI can prompt the patient to seek urgent care while still offering the earliest available booking slot. Triage logic is set up during onboarding.

Will my dermatology patients know they're speaking to an AI?

STOAIX uses a natural British English voice that is calm and professional. Patient satisfaction across clinics shows no material difference in experience versus a human receptionist for booking calls. Clinics may choose to disclose AI use if preferred.

Is STOAIX compliant with UK GDPR for handling patient data?

Yes. STOAIX is built with UK GDPR compliance in mind. Call data is processed and stored securely, with appropriate data processing agreements in place. The platform is designed for CQC-registered private practices.
