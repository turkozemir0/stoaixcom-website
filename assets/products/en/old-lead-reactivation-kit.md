# Old Lead Reactivation Kit

## Purpose

This kit is built for clinics that want to revisit leads from the last 6-24 months and turn quiet records back into live conversations.

## Segment the List First

### Segment 1: Asked About Price, Then Disappeared

- Previously showed strong interest
- Contact this group first

### Segment 2: Asked Questions, Then Went Quiet

- Still has warming potential
- Needs clarity and reassurance

### Segment 3: Said "Bad Timing"

- May be more ready now
- Needs a soft reopening

### Segment 4: Long-Silent Leads

- Use a low-pressure final check-in
- Archive if there is still no response

## Reopening Messages

1. Hi, you reached out to us a while back and I wanted to check in in case this is still something you’re considering.
2. Hi, we spoke previously and I thought it might be helpful to reconnect if your plans are still active.
3. Hi, I know some time has passed, but I wanted to see whether you’d like to revisit the conversation.
4. Hi, if the timing is better now than it was before, I’d be happy to help you continue.
