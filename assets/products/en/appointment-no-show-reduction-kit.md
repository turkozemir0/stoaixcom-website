# Appointment No-Show Reduction Kit

## Recommended Flow

- Immediate booking confirmation
- 48-hour reminder
- 24-hour final confirmation
- Same-day short reminder
- Rebooking message if the patient does not attend

## 1. Immediate Confirmation

1. Hi, your appointment has been booked. Your scheduled date and time are: ...
2. Hi, just confirming that your appointment is now in our system.
3. Hi, we’re looking forward to seeing you. If anything changes before then, just let us know.

## 2. 48-Hour Reminder

4. Hi, this is a reminder about your appointment in two days. If all still looks good, a quick confirmation is appreciated.
5. Hi, just a quick reminder that your appointment is coming up shortly. Happy to help if you need anything before then.
6. Hi, your appointment is approaching. If the scheduled time still works for you, feel free to confirm here.

## 3. 24-Hour Confirmation

7. Hi, this is your final confirmation reminder for tomorrow’s appointment. If the time still suits you, please let us know.
8. Hi, we’ll be expecting you tomorrow. If your plans have changed, just message us and we can help.
9. Hi, a quick reminder ahead of tomorrow’s appointment. Please let us know if anything needs adjusting.
