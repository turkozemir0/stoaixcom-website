Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026  |  STOAIX Ltd, London, UK

This Cookie Policy explains how STOAIX Ltd ("STOAIX", "we") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit stoaix.com. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They help the site remember your preferences, measure how you use the site, and show you relevant content.

Cookies We Use

Category Cookie / Technology Purpose Duration
Essential Session cookie, auth token Keep you logged in, security Session / 30 days
Analytics Google Analytics (_ga, _gid) Understand how visitors use the site (anonymised) Up to 2 years
Marketing Meta Pixel (_fbp) Measure ad performance, retargeting 90 days
Functional partner_ref Track affiliate referral source 45 days

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They cannot be disabled without breaking core features such as login and checkout.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns (pages visited, session duration, traffic sources). Data is anonymised — we do not identify individual users.

Marketing Cookies

We use the Meta Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our advertising on Facebook and Instagram, and to show relevant ads to people who have visited our site. You can opt out via Facebook's cookie settings.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings — most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies.
  • Our cookie banner when you first visit the site.
  • Emailing us at hello@stoaix.com to withdraw consent.

Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to browse the site, but may reduce the relevance of ads you see elsewhere.

Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
